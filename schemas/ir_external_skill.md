# IR & External Relations Audit

## Overview

This skill performs a comprehensive audit of a company's Investor Relations (IR) and External Relations posture across five critical dimensions:

1. **Governance** — Board composition, committees, ESG disclosure, regulatory compliance, and IR function maturity
2. **Investor Base** — Ownership structure, investor sentiment, activist risk, long-term vs. short-term orientation
3. **Valuation & Comparables** — Current valuation, key multiples, peer analysis, trading range assessment
4. **Commitments vs. Performance** — Guidance accuracy, strategic promise tracking, credibility assessment
5. **External Reputation** — Media sentiment, analyst coverage, stakeholder trust, awards, controversies

## Deliverables

### 1. Structured JSON Output
A comprehensive JSON file containing all audit data, scores, assessments, and recommendations organized by section.

### 2. Branded Excel Workbook
A polished, professionally branded Excel file (.xlsx) using Upscale brand colours (Eerie Black #191919, Lime Green #34C52A, Dark Cyan #429792, Ivory #FEFFEA) and Lexend font throughout.

**Excel Sheets:**
- **Overview** — Scorecard for all 5 sections, executive summary, top priorities
- **→ Governance** — Board composition, ESG, transparency, IR maturity, strengths/weaknesses/recommendations
- **→ Investor Base** — Ownership, investor segments, sentiment, activist risk, strengths/weaknesses/recommendations
- **→ Valuation** — Current valuation, key multiples with peer comparables table
- **→ Commitments** — Guidance tracking table with colour-coded beat/met/missed, strategic promises, credibility
- **→ Reputation** — Media sentiment, analyst coverage, stakeholder trust, controversies, strengths/weaknesses/recommendations
- **Dynamics** — Cross-section dynamics and interconnections

## Input Modes

Users can provide company data in three ways:

1. **Company Name** — Skill performs web research to gather data
2. **Pasted Text** — User supplies analysis, reports, or information directly
3. **Uploaded File** — User uploads company documents, reports, or analyst materials

## Scoring & Assessment

Each section is scored on a **1-10 scale**, with qualitative assessments:

- **Governance** — Board quality, ESG maturity, transparency, IR function effectiveness
- **Investor Base** — Quality of investors, sentiment, stability, alignment
- **Valuation & Comparables** — Valuation fairness, multiple positioning, peer alignment
- **Commitments vs. Performance** — Guidance accuracy, promise fulfillment, credibility
- **External Reputation** — Media/analyst favorability, stakeholder trust, controversies

## Quality Checklist

Before delivering the audit:

- [ ] All five sections completed with scores and assessments
- [ ] Governance: board diversity, ESG frameworks, IR maturity documented
- [ ] Investor Base: ownership structure, sentiment, activist risk assessed
- [ ] Valuation & Comparables: peer group identified, multiples compared, trading range analyzed
- [ ] Commitments vs. Performance: guidance history tracked, promises assessed, credibility rated
- [ ] External Reputation: media, analyst, and stakeholder perspectives included
- [ ] Recommendations: actionable with priority, effort, and timeframe estimates
- [ ] JSON file properly formatted and complete
- [ ] Excel workbook: branded, all sheets populated, tables formatted, colours applied
- [ ] Cross-section dynamics identified and documented
- [ ] Overall IR maturity assessed (world_class / advanced / competent / developing / basic)

## Frameworks & References

The audit references industry standards and best practices:

- ESG Disclosure Frameworks (SASB, GRI, TCFD, ISSB)
- Governance Standards (CG Code, SOX, Dodd-Frank)
- Analyst & Investor Best Practices (NIRI, ICGN)
- Valuation Methodologies (DCF, Comparables, Precedent Transactions)
- Reputation & Stakeholder Management Frameworks
