# Commercial Model Analysis Skill

## Overview

This skill performs a comprehensive Commercial Model audit on any business, analyzing the financial engine through six key dimensions: revenue model, unit economics, margin profile, remuneration structures, marketing ROI, and scenario planning.

## Input Modes

The skill accepts input in three ways:

1. **Web Research**: Provide a company name, and the skill searches for publicly available financial and business information
2. **Pasted Content**: Paste business descriptions, financial data, or strategic documents directly
3. **File Upload**: Upload financial statements, pitch decks, or business documents for analysis

## Analysis Framework

### Six Core Sections

Each section receives a 1-10 score with detailed findings, benchmarking, and recommendations:

1. **Revenue Model** - Pricing strategies, revenue streams, customer concentration, diversification
2. **Unit Economics** - CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, CAC payback period, ARPU, churn
3. **Margin Profile** - Gross, contribution, operating, net, and EBITDA margins with trends
4. **Remuneration** - Compensation structures, incentive alignment, retention impact, benchmarking
5. **Marketing ROI** - Overall ROI, ROAS by channel, marketing efficiency, attribution models
6. **Scenario Planning** - Base, Bull, Bear cases with sensitivity analysis and breakeven assessment

### Scoring & Assessment

- **Section Scores**: 1-10 for each dimension
- **Overall Score**: Weighted composite score
- **Commercial Health**: Thriving | Healthy | Stable | Stressed | Critical
- **Benchmarking**: Industry comparisons where available

## Deliverables

### 1. Structured JSON File

Complete analysis output with:
- Executive summary
- All six sections with detailed metrics
- Top priorities across sections
- Key benchmarks and comparisons
- Cross-section dynamics and relationships

### 2. Branded Excel Workbook

Professional Excel file with Upscale brand colors and formatting:
- **Overview**: Dashboard with all scores, summary, benchmarks, priorities
- **→ Revenue Model**: Pricing strategy, streams, concentration, diversification
- **→ Unit Economics**: CAC, LTV, ratios, ARPU, churn analysis
- **→ Margin Profile**: Gross, contribution, operating, net margins with trends
- **→ Remuneration**: Compensation structure, incentive alignment, benchmarks
- **→ Marketing ROI**: Channel performance, attribution, efficiency metrics
- **→ Scenarios**: Base/Bull/Bear comparison with assumptions and metrics
- **Benchmarks**: Industry comparison table
- **Dynamics**: Cross-section insights and relationships
